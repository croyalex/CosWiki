Welcome!

Hello, this is my mockup page for what is supposed to be a cosplaying related wiki / forum website where people can find all sorts of resources such as but not limited to: tutorials, videos, ideas, and pages that consist of "how to cosplay X character". For now, this website will feature exclusively HTML & CSS, as my intention is to showcase my "artistic" side of the process of developing in front end (which is mostly about making the style feel consistent, or having it's design changed if viewed from a mobile phone's screen A.K.A. responsive websites, or even adding little animations that are not resource intensive as a small touch to give said website it's own personality). Despite using raw CSS, I could implement tools such as frameworks dedicated to front end development (such as React or Bootstrap) if required. I am also really careful when it comes to content not appearing where it is supposed to be (ex. a video player going out of bounds from the div that wraps the content).
